Frequently Asked Questions

Does AppVault host APK files for download?
No. AppVault is a discovery and review platform. We link all apps directly to their official Google Play Store listings. We never host APK files. This ensures you always download apps from a verified, safe source.
Are the app ratings on AppVault real?
Yes. All ratings, review counts, download numbers, and version information are pulled in real time from the Google Play Store via the official Play Store API. We don't fabricate or modify any of these figures.
Can I suggest an app to be featured?
Absolutely! We love hearing about apps our community uses. Email your suggestion to apps@sgappstech.com with the app name and a brief note about why you think it's worth featuring. We review every submission.
Is AppVault affiliated with Google?
No. AppVault is an independent platform. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Google LLC in any way. Google Play, Android, and related marks are trademarks of Google LLC.
How do I report a harmful or misleading app listing?
Email support@sgappstech.com with the app name and a brief description of the concern. We take these reports seriously and investigate all submissions promptly. For urgent safety concerns related to malware, we'll escalate within hours.
How quickly do you respond to messages?
We aim to respond to all messages within 24 hours on business days (Monday–Friday). For urgent issues like security reports or billing problems, we prioritize and typically respond within a few hours.
