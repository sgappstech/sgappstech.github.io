Who We Are

SG Apps Tech is a small, independent Android app studio. Rather than building one large app that tries to do everything, we focus on small, purpose-built sticker packs for WhatsApp — each one polished around a single character or theme, kept lightweight, and updated based on the feedback we get from real users through the contact page and Play Store reviews.

This page goes into more detail on how we work. For the short version, head back to our homepage, or browse the full apps gallery.

How Our Sticker Packs Are Made

Every pack starts as original artwork designed around one character — Bubu Panda, Yellow Dino, or the baby characters in our other packs. We sketch a set of expressions and reactions first (happy, sleepy, excited, confused, and so on) so the final pack covers the moments people actually use stickers for in a chat, rather than just being a random image dump. Once the artwork is final, we package it using WhatsApp's official sticker-pack format, so installation is a single tap and nothing needs to be downloaded outside the Play Store.

Why We Focus on WhatsApp Stickers

Sticker packs are a good fit for a small studio: they're small in file size (usually a few megabytes), don't need permissions like location or contacts, and can be genuinely useful without being invasive. We'd rather ship a handful of sticker packs that work well than a bloated all-in-one app that asks for permissions it doesn't need.

Our Development Priorities

✔ Small download size — our apps are typically under 10 MB
✔ No unnecessary permissions — we don't request contacts, location, or storage access beyond what WhatsApp's sticker API requires
✔ Regular content updates based on user requests
✔ Ads that support development without blocking the app's core function
✔ Every app is free to download and use
