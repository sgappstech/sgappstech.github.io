    How to Add These Stickers to WhatsApp

    1. Tap Install App above to open the app on the Google Play Store and download it.
    2. Open the app once it finishes installing on your phone.
    3. Browse the sticker pack and tap the Add to WhatsApp icon shown on the pack.
    4. WhatsApp opens automatically and asks you to confirm — tap Add.
    5. Open any WhatsApp chat, tap the sticker icon, and the new pack will appear ready to use.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is this sticker app free to use?

    Yes. All SG Apps Tech sticker packs are free to download and use, and are supported by non-intrusive advertising.

    Do I need a WhatsApp Business account?

    No. These sticker packs work with both regular WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business, since both apps use the same sticker integration provided by Android.

    Will this app access my chats or contacts?

    No. The app only hands the sticker images to WhatsApp when you choose to add them — it does not read your messages or contact list. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

    The stickers didn't appear in WhatsApp. What should I do?

    Make sure WhatsApp is updated to the latest version from the Play Store, then reopen the sticker app and tap Add to WhatsApp again. If the issue continues, contact us from our Contact page and we'll help you sort it out.
