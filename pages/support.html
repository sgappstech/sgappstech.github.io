Support

How Can We Help?

Common fixes for our sticker apps, plus how to reach us if you need more help.

📥

Installation Issues

Make sure you have a stable connection while downloading from the Play Store, and that your device has enough free storage (our apps are typically under 10 MB).

🩹

Stickers Not Appearing

Update WhatsApp to the latest version, reopen the sticker app, and tap "Add to WhatsApp" again. The pack should then appear in WhatsApp's sticker tray.

⚠️

App Crashes or Freezes

Try force-closing the app and reopening it, or restart your device. If the issue continues, uninstalling and reinstalling usually resolves it.

🔒

Privacy Questions

Our apps don't read your messages or contacts. See our full Privacy Policy for details on what's collected and why.

💡

Sticker Requests

Want a new character or theme? Send us the idea through the contact form below — it's the main way we decide what to build next.

🗑️

Uninstalling

Remove the app anytime from Settings → Apps. To remove just the stickers from WhatsApp, long-press the pack in the sticker tray and choose remove.

Still Need Help?

If none of the above solved your issue, reach out directly and we'll get back to you within 24–48 hours.
