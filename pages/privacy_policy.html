Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how SG Apps Tech collects, uses, and protects your information when using our application.

SG Apps Tech develops and publishes free Android sticker applications for WhatsApp, including Bubu Panda Stickers for WA, Yellow Dino Stickers for WA, Cute Baby Stickers for WA, and Animated Baby Stickers for WA (collectively, "the Apps"). This page explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use any of the Apps or this website.

If you choose to use our Apps, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The Personal Information that we collect is used for providing and improving the Apps. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

Our sticker apps are designed to work without requiring an account or personal profile. When you tap "Add to WhatsApp," the sticker pack is handed off directly to WhatsApp using Android's official sticker intent — we do not receive your phone number, contacts, or message content as part of that process.

The app does use third-party services, such as advertising and analytics SDKs, that may collect information used to identify your device.
