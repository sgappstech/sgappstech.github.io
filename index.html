About Us

Who We Are

SG Apps Tech is a small, independent Android studio focused on building polished, lightweight WhatsApp sticker packs — each one designed around a single character, with no unnecessary permissions and no bloated downloads.

We started out with a simple observation: most sticker apps ask for far more than they need, bundle in features nobody uses, and get abandoned after a version or two. We wanted to try the opposite approach — one character, one focused app, kept small and kept current. Bubu Panda was our first pack, and the response from users encouraged us to keep expanding the lineup with Yellow Dino, Cute Baby, and our newest animated pack.

Every app we publish goes through the same checklist: does it install quickly, does it work offline once set up, and does it respect the person using it. If an update doesn't move us closer to that, we don't ship it.

Frequently Asked Questions

A few things people ask us most often.

Are SG Apps Tech's sticker apps free?

Yes. Every app we publish is free to download and use. They're supported by non-intrusive advertising rather than subscriptions or in-app purchases.

Do the apps work without an internet connection?

Once installed, the sticker packs work fully offline — you don't need a connection to browse or send stickers in WhatsApp. A connection is only used to display ads and download the app itself.

Do you collect my personal data?

Our sticker apps don't require an account and don't read your messages or contacts. Some anonymous device information is collected by third-party advertising and analytics SDKs. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
